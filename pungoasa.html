Users are giving feedback

Had to play around at first to figure it out, but it's now easy to log my daily meals on to enter goals and then track calories, carbs, protein and all other nutrients. Only wish they tracked glycemic levels of foods. Inputting my weight, exercise and water each day is easy. Putting recipes in was clunky at first and I'm still not sure I did it right, but it works for me. Like it a lot.

Nancy Montgomery

The layout is kinda clumsy for mobile. It's more like an afterthought from a computer-based design. It needs to be designed to flow more intuitively. Like why is Progress laid out separately? Progress is the ultimate goal of this app. As such, it should be readily available anywhere throughout the app. As a pop-up/hot-key at the top of the screen. You want people to be able to see and boast about their progress ASAP. And what's with videos not playing from the "News Feed"? It hangs!

Dean Coronado

Used to have my fitness pal years ago, and it was great. So simple, so effective, recommended it to many. Thought I'd give it another try, and it's awful. Everytime I try to add food, it asks me if my goals are OK, with the box ticked for giving me updates (which I don't want), then sends me to the free upgrade page (which I also do not want). Relentless. Can't use the app in its simple form. Have had it two days, and will be uninstalling it. Very disappointed. Why change a good thing??

Anna Randall

Great app. Very detailed. But I'm a student. No money to pay for premium. Would consider once I have a full-time job! Except every time I open the app it asks me 5 times to upgrade! Then it goes through the goals and notifications settings!? So I have to tap 15 times (with serious lag between each one) just to get to my diary! And then very regularly in the middle of making a meal/recipe, it'll randomly do it again! It's GOT to be fixed or I won't be keeping the app long enough to get premium!
